Everything Stone Mountain Offers

City Services & Resources

The City of Stone Mountain delivers a comprehensive range of municipal services to every resident and business in our community. From utilities to public safety, every department is committed to professionalism, responsiveness, and the wellbeing of our neighbors.

Utilities & Infrastructure

Stone Mountain is committed to delivering reliable, high-quality utility services to every address in the city — with modern online access and responsive customer support.

Water & Sewer Services

Safe, clean water and wastewater treatment for all Stone Mountain households and businesses. Online billing is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Sanitation & Solid Waste

Weekly curbside collection for household waste and recyclables, bulk item pickup scheduling, and responsible solid waste management across the city.

Streets & Public Works

Road maintenance, pothole repair, stormwater management, street lighting, and sidewalk improvements throughout all Stone Mountain neighborhoods.

Building Permits & Inspections

Residential and commercial building permits, code enforcement, and inspection scheduling for all construction and renovation projects within city limits.

Community & Civic Services

Stone Mountain's community is its greatest asset. The city invests deeply in the safety, wellbeing, and quality of life of every resident — from our youngest families to our longest-standing neighbors.

Police Department

The Stone Mountain Police Department provides 24/7 professional law enforcement, community policing initiatives, and emergency response services to protect every resident and visitor.

Fire & Emergency Services

Rapid-response fire suppression, rescue operations, and emergency medical services dedicated to protecting every Stone Mountain family and property.

Parks & Recreation

Stone Mountain's parks system provides athletic facilities, trails, playgrounds, and community gathering spaces — enriching the quality of life for residents of every age.

Planning, Zoning & Heritage

Land use planning, zoning administration, historic district guidelines, and variance reviews — ensuring responsible growth that honors Stone Mountain's irreplaceable character and heritage.

About Mayor Jones

Rooted in Stone Mountain.
Committed to Its People.

Mayor Jelani Linder serves the City of Stone Mountain, Georgia with a genuine and abiding love for this community — its people, its history, and the extraordinary landmark that has defined it for generations. Elected to lead one of DeKalb County's most distinctive cities, Mayor Jones brings to office a deep well of civic experience, a strong network of community relationships, and an unwavering commitment to the families who call Stone Mountain home.

Mayor Jones understands that Stone Mountain is more than a city — it is a community with a character all its own. Nestled alongside one of the natural wonders of the American South, Stone Mountain Village has long drawn residents who value both the beauty of their surroundings and the warmth of a true neighborhood. Mayor Jones is dedicated to honoring that character in every decision made from City Hall.

Her priorities are clear: responsive city services that residents can count on, infrastructure investments that keep neighborhoods safe and beautiful, economic vitality that supports local businesses and brings new opportunity to Main Street, and open, transparent governance that gives every resident a clear line of sight into how their city is managed.

Mayor Jones believes that the strength of Stone Mountain lies in the diversity, resilience, and community spirit of its people. Her door is always open — because she knows that the best ideas for this city come from the residents who live it every day.

Mayor's Strategic Priorities

Preserving and celebrating Stone Mountain's historic heritage & village character
Infrastructure investment — roads, sidewalks, parks, and public spaces
Economic development & support for local businesses on Main Street
Public safety & community-centered policing for every neighborhood
Transparent, accessible governance that works for every Stone Mountain family
